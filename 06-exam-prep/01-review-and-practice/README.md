# Review and Practice

**Part of section:** Exam Prep
**Estimated time:** 5 hours
**Prerequisites:** Sections 01–05 complete
**Exam mapping:** Not scored — review and rehearsal across all five domains

## Overview

This module is the practical machinery of exam prep: a structured review of the blueprint to confirm
coverage and expose gaps, a timed mock-exam-and-pacing rehearsal, and a consolidated pitfalls-and-
checklist chapter for the final days before the test.

## Learning Outcomes

By completing this module, you will be able to:

- Verify your coverage of every exam objective and produce a targeted remediation plan
- Rehearse the exam under realistic timing with a repeatable question-triage process
- Walk into the exam with the common traps and a final checklist internalized

## Topics Covered

- Objective-by-objective blueprint coverage map and honest gap-analysis self-assessment
- Mock exam practice questions with answers, plus timing/pacing and triage strategy
- Common pitfalls, renamed-product distractors, and a final knowledge + logistics checklist

## How This Module Fits

It's the only module in Section 06 and the last content in the repo. Each chapter points back into
Sections 01–05 for remediation, so treat it as an index-and-rehearsal layer over everything you've
learned rather than standalone material.

## Study Tips

- Work the three chapters **in order**: review/gap-analysis → timed mock → pitfalls/checklist. The
  first finds your weak spots, the second pressure-tests them, the third patches the last leaks.
- Revisit this module twice: once early (to plan remediation) and once in the final 48 hours (to
  rehearse and run the checklist).
